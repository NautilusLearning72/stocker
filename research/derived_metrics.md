# Derived & Analytical Metrics for Investment Analysts and Traders

## 1. Personas and Their Metrics

### Persona A --- Fundamental Analyst

-   P/E, Forward P/E
-   PEG Ratio
-   EV/EBITDA
-   Free Cash Flow Yield
-   ROIC, ROE
-   Altman Z-Score
-   Piotroski F-Score
-   Dividend payout metrics

### Persona B --- Quant Researcher / Factor Investor

-   Value factors: Book-to-market, Earnings yield, Cash flow yield
-   Quality factors: Gross profitability, ROIC, leverage
-   Momentum: 1M, 3M, 6M, 12M momentum
-   Volatility: Beta, idiosyncratic vol, ATR
-   Growth: Revenue/EPS growth
-   Sentiment: news, social, earnings sentiment

### Persona C --- Technical Trader

-   Trend: SMA/EMA, MACD, ADX, Ichimoku
-   Volatility: Bollinger Bands, ATR, HV
-   Price Action: RSI, Stochastics, CCI
-   Volume: VWAP, OBV, Accum/Dist

### Persona D --- Short-Term Quant

-   Realized volatility (intraday)
-   Bid-ask spread
-   Order flow imbalance
-   VWAP deviation

### Persona E --- Sentiment-Driven Trader

-   Sentiment momentum
-   News volume score
-   Sentiment surprise
-   Earnings call sentiment
